Interior Design Cost In Mumbai

A detailed 2026 guide to realistic budgets, room-wise costing, and hidden charges before you begin your project.

If you are planning a home in Mumbai, budget clarity is the single most important step before design starts. This guide breaks down interior design cost in Mumbai with practical ranges for 1BHK, 2BHK, 3BHK, and larger homes, so you can avoid under-budgeting and costly rework.

Average Interior Design Cost In Mumbai (2026)

In Mumbai, end-to-end interior cost usually depends on scope, custom furniture volume, and finish quality. As a practical benchmark, turnkey interiors often range between INR 2,200 and INR 5,500+ per sq ft. Entry-level packages can be lower, while premium and fully bespoke projects can go significantly higher.

1BHK

INR 5-10 lakh for practical essentials; INR 12-18 lakh for premium finish.

2BHK

INR 8-12 lakh for essentials; INR 18-30+ lakh for premium turnkey execution.

3BHK / 4BHK

INR 15-25 lakh mid-tier; INR 35 lakh onward for high-end luxury custom design.

What Impacts Interior Design Cost The Most?

  • Material specification: Marine plywood, veneers, acrylic, laminates, stone, and hardware grades can shift budgets quickly.
  • Customization level: Fully custom carpentry costs more than modular standards but gives better fit in compact Mumbai layouts.
  • Civil and MEP changes: Re-routing electrical, plumbing, AC, and false ceiling layouts adds execution complexity.
  • Timeline: Fast-track delivery usually increases labor and logistics costs.
  • Brand selection: Kitchen accessories, wardrobes, lighting, and smart controls vary widely by brand tier.

Room-Wise Budget Breakdown

Living Room

TV wall, storage, lighting, and ceiling treatments often make the living room one of the highest visibility spends. Keep 15-25% of your total budget here for strong first impact.

Kitchen

Modular kitchen cost can vary massively based on shutter material, tandem systems, countertop type, and appliance integration. For many Mumbai homes, this is the single biggest line item.

Bedrooms + Wardrobes

Wardrobe depth planning, loft usage, and internal accessories directly affect both storage quality and spend. Smart planning here improves long-term usability more than decorative upgrades.

How To Choose The Right Budget Tier

  • Essential Tier: Prioritize layout, storage, and durable basics. Ideal for rental or quick move-in projects.
  • Balanced Tier: Mix of practical materials with selected premium highlights for better look and longevity.
  • Luxury Tier: Bespoke detailing, advanced lighting, premium finishes, and integrated smart systems.

Hidden Costs Homeowners Often Miss

  • Society deposits and working-hour restrictions
  • Debris removal, shift protection, and site logistics
  • Last-mile design changes during execution
  • Premium hardware upgrades after site trials
  • Soft furnishing add-ons (curtains, rugs, decor lighting)
