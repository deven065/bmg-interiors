Who We Are

Where Art Meets
Architecture

BMG Interiors was born from a singular conviction — that the spaces we inhabit shape who we are. What started as a boutique Mumbai studio in 2014 has grown into one of India's most respected interior design firms with 150+ projects and 40+ industry awards.

Our founder's philosophy is simple: design is never decoration. Every BMG project starts with the person who will live or work in the space and works backwards from there — understanding their rituals, aspirations and the way they want to feel when they come home.

We partner with India's finest craftsmen, source materials globally and bring an editorial eye to every surface, fixture and detail. The result is always a home or office that feels authentically, completely yours.
