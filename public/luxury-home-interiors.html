What Defines a Luxury Interior?

At BMG Interiors, we believe luxury is defined by **Tailored Exclusivity**. Every element should be curated to the homeowner's lifestyle. It's the difference between a house that looks expensive and a home that feels like a masterpiece. True luxury involves:

  • Spatial Harmony: Maximizing the sense of volume, even in compact Mumbai apartments.
  • Micro-Detailing: From the stitch on a leather-clad wardrobe to the mitre joint of an Italian marble floor.
  • Sensory Experience: Using textures like silk, velvet, raw stone, and polished metal to create a multi-sensory environment.

The Palette of Opulence: Premium Materials

The foundation of luxury lies in the authenticity of materials. We source the finest elements globally to ensure your home stands the test of time and trends.

Italian Marbles

Statuary, Calacatta, and Nero Marquina. We hand-pick slabs from quarries to ensure perfect book-matching and vein alignment.

Exotic Veneers

Smoked oak, walnut, and eucalyptus veneers treated with high-end PU or lamination for a glass-like finish.

Metals & Accents

PVD-coated stainless steel, brushed brass, and champagne gold accents to add a touch of regality to wall panels and furniture.

Luxury Fabrics

Belgium velvets, Italian linens, and suede finish leathers that provide tactile comfort and visual depth.

Bespoke Furniture & Architectural Joinery

In a luxury home, off-the-shelf is rarely an option. Our in-house designers and veteran craftsmen create furniture that fits your space like a bespoke suit.

The BMG Signature: Whether it's a floating TV console with seamless stone capping or a walkthrough closet with sensor-driven internal lighting, our joinery is built for both aesthetics and ergonomic perfection.

Lighting: The Invisible Luxury

Lighting is the "jewelry" of the home. We integrate layered lighting systems—ambient, task, and accent—controlled by state-of-the-art automation.

  • Magnetic Track Lighting: For flexible, minimal ceiling designs.
  • Cove & Accentuation: To highlight textures and art pieces.
  • Smart Home Integration: Control your lighting scenes, climate, and security with a single touch or voice command.

Turnkey Luxury: Design to Handover

Our turnkey approach ensures that the design vision is executed flawlessly. We manage everything from civil modifications and MEP re-routing to the final placement of decor pieces. This allows our clients to enjoy the creative process while we handle the complexities of on-site execution.
