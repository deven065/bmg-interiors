The Vision

A bespoke family home where artisan craftsmanship meets quiet luxury — every detail curated with intention to reflect the family's distinctive personality.

From the hand-carved stone accents to the smart home integration, the Mehta Residence represents the pinnacle of modern residential design in Mumbai. We focused on open transitions and refined aesthetics to create a peaceful sanctuary.

Materials include high-end veneers, custom brass hardware, and specialized lighting systems designed to enhance the layering of spaces during day and night.
