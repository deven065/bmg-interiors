Featured Project: A Bandra West Residence

A young family in Bandra West came to BMG Interiors with a 3BHK that had strong bones but a generic finish. Their brief: make it feel like a premium boutique hotel while keeping it a real family home. We opened up the living and dining zone, introduced a palette of warm whites, brushed oak, and handcrafted ceramic accents, and redesigned the kitchen with integrated appliances and an island that doubles as a breakfast counter.

The master bedroom was reimagined as a serene private suite — layered lighting, a bespoke walk-in wardrobe, and a bathroom finished in large-format stone slabs. The children's rooms were designed with clever, concealed storage and flexible furniture layouts to grow with the family. The final result was a home that felt completely bespoke, entirely liveable, and genuinely extraordinary.
