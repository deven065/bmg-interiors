Privacy Policy

At BMG Interiors, we hold your trust as our most valuable asset. This policy details our commitment to protecting your personal information.

Last Updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

BMG Interiors ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is dedicated to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you interact with our studio, whether through our physical offices, website, social media channels, or turnkey services.

Information We Collect

To provide the high-quality, bespoke design services BMG Interiors is known for, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact details: Name, phone number, email address, and postal address.
  • Project Specifications: Site address, site measurements, shared photos/videos, design preferences, budget ranges, and project timelines.
  • Communications: Records of your messages, emails, call notes, and support inquiries.
  • Financial Information: Invoices, payment status, and transaction references (please note we do not store full credit card details; these are processed securely by third-party providers).
  • Digital Data: If you visit our website, we may collect IP addresses, device identifiers, browser types, pages viewed, and cookies.

How We Use Your Information

We utilize your information to deliver an exceptional client experience and fulfill our professional obligations:

  • To respond to inquiries and provide comprehensive quotes or estimates.
  • To plan, design, and execute interior design and turnkey projects.
  • To coordinate site visits, manage vendors, and oversee project milestones.
  • To issue invoices, process payments, and maintain accurate accounting records.
  • To refine our services and enhance our digital customer experience.
  • To comply with legal requirements and protect our studio against fraud or misuse.

Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable, we process personal information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Your Consent: Where you have specifically agreed to the processing.
  • Contractual Necessity: To perform the services requested during your design journey.
  • Legitimate Interests: To operate and improve our business operations.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with statutory or regulatory requirements.

Sharing and Disclosure

We share information only when necessary to facilitate your project:

  • Specialized Contractors: Installers, carpenters, electricians, plumbers, architects, and photographers involved in your project.
  • Strategic Partners: Technology providers (cloud storage, accounting tools) who process data on our behalf.
  • Secure Payment Processors: To facilitate seamless financial transactions.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Cookies and Digital Tracking

Our website may use cookies to improve functionality and analyze traffic patterns. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, though some features of our site may be affected if cookies are disabled.

Data Retention and Security

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined above, including legal and operational requirements. We employ robust administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. However, please be aware that no transmission method over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and review copies of the personal information we hold.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal limitations.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.
